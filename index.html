Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Offline Reader ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information. Offline Reader is a local document and ebook reader application designed to open and render PDF, EPUB, MOBI, AZW3, and Comic (CBZ) files.

1. Zero Data Collection

We believe in absolute privacy. Offline Reader operates 100% offline. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage statistics, device identifiers, or user data. No sign-up, registration, or online account is required to use the application.

2. Local Storage and Files

All books, documents, drawings, highlights, and annotations are saved exclusively on your local device. The application does not upload your files or annotations to any remote server or cloud service.

3. Permissions We Request

To function correctly as an offline document manager, the application requests the following permissions:

  • All Files Access (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Used strictly to scan your local storage directories to discover, catalog, and display your PDF, EPUB, MOBI, AZW3, and Comic (CBZ) files in the library. This scanning process runs entirely on your device with no internet access.

4. No Third-Party Integration

We do not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs, analytic trackers, or background monitoring tools that collect your information. Your reading history, files, and activities remain completely private to you.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly on this page with an updated effective date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: support@traudio.xyz.
